About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer currently specializing in Front End Development. I always had an interest in creating content for the web and joining the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Development gave me the skills I have today along with the ability to evolve them on my own.

Although my short term goal is to get more in depth knowledge on JavaScript and CSS, what I want to do long term is to learn more about - and eventually expand and normalize - accessibility on the web. Making the internet more accessible can greatly improve the experiences of hundreds of millions of people with impairments.
